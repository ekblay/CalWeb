Missions and Outreach

Jesus Said To Go. He taught us to leave our comfort zones and cross barriers like mountains, oceans, and streets. He commissioned us to reach across the aisle, culture, social class and political borders.
He asks that we overcome the divisions of, language, colour and creed, to share the good news. We are committed to go when called, give when prompted and pray always to fulfill our responsibility to Him.
