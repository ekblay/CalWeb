Media Ministries

The purpose of the Media Ministry at Calvary Church is to carry the Gospel of Jesus Christ to our visually-driven, ever-changing world through the use of accessible technology and media

It is also an avenue to equip other units of ministries of the church to use digital and electronic media as a worship and outreach tool. By this we are aiming to enhance the worship experience, communicating with the congregation, extending the congregation beyond the walls of the church, and with this propagating the Kingdom of our LORD and his Christ.
