Young Adults

Young Adults is a group of college and career aged individuals in our congregation that get together and connect with each other.

The most lasting friendships are formed during these years and we strive to create an environment to facilitate those friendships within a safe, faith based place.
OUR FOCUS is Journeying together with others and with God proclaiming the Work and message of Christ's love and helping others discover their purpose in life as well.
