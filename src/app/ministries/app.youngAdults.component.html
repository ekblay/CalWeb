Young Adults is a group of college and career aged individuals in our congregation that get together and
connect with each other.
The most lasting friendships are formed during these years and we strive to create an environment
to facilitate those friendships within a safe, faith based place.
OUR FOCUS is Journeying together with and with God and proclaiming the Work and message of
Christ's love and helping others discover their purpose in life as well.
If you have any questions or would like to be plugged in to this ministry,
reach out to the youth team.
