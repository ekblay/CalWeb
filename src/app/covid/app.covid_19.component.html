We are pleased to see that we are getting closer to that time where all Covid restrictions will be lifted. Until then, we must continue to wear masks in the church at all times, and practice physical distancing where able.

Let us please be in prayer for our ministry opportunities here in our church, our community, and around the world.
This pandemic does not stop the great commission from being carried out to the Glory of God!
